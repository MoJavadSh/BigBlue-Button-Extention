‌BigBlue Control

How to Use

2.5x Speed: Sets the video playback speed to 2.5x.

3x Speed: Sets the video playback speed to 3x.

Enable Keys: Activates keyboard controls:

  • Space: Play/Pause
  • Arrow Right: Skip 15 seconds forward
  • Arrow Left: Skip 15 seconds backward
